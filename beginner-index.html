Essential Cooking Tips

Knife Safety

Always keep your fingers curled under when cutting. Start with a sharp knife - it's actually safer!

Temperature Control

Start with medium heat and adjust as needed. It's easier to increase heat than to cool down a burnt pan.

Seasoning

Start with a little salt and taste as you go. You can always add more, but you can't take it away!

Timing

Read the entire recipe before you start. Prep all ingredients first - this is called "mise en place".
